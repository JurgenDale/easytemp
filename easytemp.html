Frequently Asked Question

Can I use my content for clients and commercial use?

Yes. Pro Users can use any document, design, or form templates from Template.net for personal, professional, or commercial use.

Do I have to give attribution/credit?

Pro users do not have to attribute or give credit to use our templates. As a Pro user you will have full access to all our content.

Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes. You can cancel your subscription anytime from your Dashboard or by contacting our support team. We will not charge you any further, and you can continue to use/download our templates until the last date of your subscription.

Is my data private?

Yes. We host all our content on Amazon AWS secure cloud in North America. All payments are fully encrypted &handled by Stripe. We do not store your card details on our servers, and we have a strict security, usage policy &technology in place for unfair, abusive users.

Can I contact support anytime?

Yes. Pro users can get 24/7, 365 days support from our team via email, chat &phone.

Can I add team members?

Yes. Upgrade to a Business plan and add up to 5 team members for free. You can easily add your team members straight from your Dashboard.
